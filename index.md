## 📌 About this application

denidian stores your thoughts in simple text files. You keep control of your notes because they sit directly on your computer hardware. It includes features for linking notes together and visualizing your connections in a workspace map.

## ✍️ Creating your first note

The interface provides a clean canvas. To create your first note, select the New Note button in the top left corner. Type your text directly into the main window. The program saves your work automatically as you type. You can format your text using simple symbols. For example, place a hashtag before a word to make it a header, or put brackets around words to turn them into links.

## 🔗 Linking your thoughts

The strength of denidian lies in how it connects information. When you want to reference another note, type two square brackets around the target word, like this: `[[Important Project]]`. The application creates a link to a file with that name. If the file does not exist yet, the program creates it the moment you click that link. This creates a web of knowledge unique to your specific interests.

## 🌐 Navigating the graph view

You can visualize your notes through the graph view. Access this by selecting the graph icon in the sidebar. You see a spread of dots where each dot represents a single note. Lines connect dots that share a link. Drag the dots with your mouse to rearrange them or click a dot to open the underlying file. This view helps you find hidden connections between topics you document over time.

## 🛡️ Managing your data

Your data stays in a folder on your local drive. To change where the program saves your notes, open the Settings menu and select the Folder location tab. Choose a folder backed up by services like Dropbox or OneDrive if you want to keep copies of your notes on multiple machines. You can copy these text files to any other computer and edit them with other programs if needed.

## 🔧 Adjusting program settings

The settings menu allows you to modify the look and feel of your experience. You can switch between light mode and dark mode to reduce eye strain. You can also change the font size to make text easier to read. These changes apply immediately to your current session.
